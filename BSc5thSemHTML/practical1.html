About


Department of Computer Science of the College was started in 1995 . Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra also served the College and Dept. in its beginnings. In 21st century, the teaching learning of the subject has been universalized in all classes and streams of institution. The department currently offers UG programs as BCA and B.Sc in Computer Science and PG Diploma in Computer Applications.
